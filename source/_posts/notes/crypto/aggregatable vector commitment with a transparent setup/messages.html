Given a single commitment c, and k evaluations with their proofs. I want an aggregator with no knowledge of the decomitment of c to be able to compress [pi_1...pi_k] for [(x_1, y_1) ... (x_k, y_k)] into one proof pi. The verification function would take C, the k points, the compressed proof and return a bool. I believe this is called same commitment aggregation or subvector commitments. Hyperproofs and point proofs does this but they are not transparent (they need a trusted setup).
